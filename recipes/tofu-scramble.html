Tofu Scramble

The perfect replacement for scrambled eggs

This quick and hearty breakfast is loaded with protien and the perfect way to start your day.

Ingrediants

  • Olive oil
  • Hashbrowns
  • Half a brick of tofu (pressed)
  • 1 onion
  • 1 clove of garlic
  • Mushrooms
  • 1 Bell pepper
  • 1 big handful Spinich
  • 1 table spoon Garam masala
  • 1 table spoon Tumeric
  • 1 table spoon Nutritonal yeast

directions

  1. In a large pan heat up some olive oil and prepare your vegetables by washing and dicing them
  2. Add your garlic, onion and hashbrowns to the pan and cook for 5-10 minutes until onions are translucent
  3. Add in your mushrooms and pepper and cook until they begin to soften up
  4. With your hands crumble up your tofu into small pieces directly into the pan
  5. Add in your garam masala, tumeric, and nutritional yeast and mix well
  6. after cooking for another 5 minutes add in your spinich and turn off the heat keeping the pan on the element and continue to stir until the spinish is mixed in
  7. serve with toast or by its self
