Vegan Protein Shake

High in protein and delicious

This tasty shake is the perfect after gym snack

Ingrediants

  • 1 Scoop of chocolate or vanilla protein powder
  • 2 Big spoonfuls of peanut butter
  • 1 Banana
  • 1 Cup of milk

Directions

  1. Combine all ingrediants in a blender and top off with water or ice
  2. Blend and enjoy
