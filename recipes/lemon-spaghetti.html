Lemon Spaghetti

A quick tasty pasta dish perfect for two

This delicious pasta dish is perfect for those in a rush. with just a few ingrediants this chessy and lemony dish can be yours in under 20 minutes

ingrediants

  • spaghetti
  • 1 clove of garlic
  • half an onion
  • parsley
  • nutrional yeast
  • salt
  • peper
  • olive oil

steps

  1. in a large pan heat up some oil and put a large pot of water on to boil thats been generously salted
  2. chop up your garlic and onion into small bits and saute on the pan for about 5 minutes adding your spaghetti to the water when it boils
  3. once the garlic and onions is coocked splash a little of the pasta water into the pan to stop the cooking
  4. when the pasta is one minute from being done remove the pasta and add it to the pan
  5. squeeze the juice of a large lemon and add two scoops of nutrional yeast into the pan and stir
  6. serve and top with pepper,chopped parsley, and more nutrional yeast
